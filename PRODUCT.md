# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective collaborators, clients, employers, product users, and peers who want a fast, credible understanding of Mithun's work. They may arrive on desktop or mobile, in daylight or a dim room, and should be able to scan the page without learning a new interaction model.

## Product Purpose

Present Mithun as the founder of Chevvi and a hands-on software engineer who ships useful AI products. The site should make Owlish and Mikan AI tangible, establish experience through recognizable company names, and create clear paths to the products and professional profiles.

## Brand Personality

Deliberate, technical, quietly inventive. The page should feel confident and human, with enough crafted motion to be memorable without turning the portfolio into a visual demo reel.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page gradients, glassmorphism, ornamental device mockups, novelty cursor effects, generic unlabeled switches, cropped product screenshots, and motion scattered across every section.

## Design Principles

- Let shipped work provide the proof.
- Use one memorable technical gesture, then keep the rest restrained.
- Preserve legibility and product fidelity in both themes.
- Make every external destination obvious and trustworthy.
- Prefer precise framing and spacing over decorative containers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard behavior. Respect system theme, persist explicit theme choices, provide an unambiguous theme label, and replace continuous motion with a static composition when reduced motion is requested. Never depend on color alone to communicate state.
